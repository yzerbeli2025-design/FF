      About Me

      My name is Zarbali Yusuf Ilham oglu. I was born on January 2, 2008 in Qaratapa village, Sabirabad district, Azerbaijan.

      From a very early age I was known as a calm, kind, intelligent and tidy child. I started talking at just one year old, and by one and a half I could already recite poems, fairy tales, and even surahs from the Holy Quran by heart.

      Before starting school I already knew how to read and write. My pre-school teacher, Zərife Heyderova, played a big role in my early development. I currently study at School No. 25.

      My favourite subjects are Mathematics, English, and Life Science. I regularly participate in knowledge competitions and enjoy playing chess and solving logic puzzles in my free time.

      My greatest joy is spending time with my family. My father, İlham bəy, takes us on trips around Azerbaijan's beautiful regions every summer and to cinemas, theatres and entertainment centres in winter.

      My role model is my father. I promised him I would study hard and become a highly skilled surgeon. I plan to continue my education abroad and master several foreign languages.

      If history remembers me, I want to be remembered as a patriotic son like Mubariz Ibrahimov, Ramil Safarov, and Chingiz Mustafayev — people who left a true mark on history.

      I am now a proud student of the IT Faculty at Baku Engineering University, taking the first steps toward my big dreams. 🎓

      "I want to leave a mark on history — not just as a surgeon, but as a patriot."
      — Yusuf Zarbali
